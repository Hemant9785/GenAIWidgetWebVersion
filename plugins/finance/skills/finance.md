# Finance Skill (Mock)

This skill describes finance capabilities and rules.

## Capabilities

### Stock Price Checking
- Look up current trading prices for stock tickers (e.g. GOOG, MSFT, AAPL).
- Display price change percentage.
- Render charts of historical close data.

### Portfolio Performance
- Summarize overall portfolio value.
- List individual stock holdings.
- Display distribution badges.
